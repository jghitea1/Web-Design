The Fire Department

The Glendive Fire Department consists of 4 full-time firefighters, permanent part time fire manager,
and permanent volunteer fire chief with 13 paid-per-call volunteers.

Our primary response area covers 3.5 square miles. The Glendive Fire Department is housed in one fire station and operates two frontline pumpers,
one 75 foot aerial, one command vehicle and one wildland pumper.

The Finance Department is responsible for the following functions:

  • Preparation of the Annual Budget and Annual Financial Report

  • Debt management

  • Investments

  • Administration of the accounting system including accounts payable, payroll, and special assessments

  • Assessment management for lighting, sprinkling, and curb/sidewalk districts

  • Grant writing and grant monitoring

  • Financing fixed assets and major projects

  • Payroll preparation and administration

  • Claims administration

  • Personnel Policies and Human Resources

  • Risk Management

  • City records including minutes, resolutions, and ordinances

  • Council minutes, packets, and legal notices

  • City licensing
